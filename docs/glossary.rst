Glossary
========

.. glossary::

   ABC/2
      Formula for estimating volume of intracerebral hemorrhage from CT: V = A×B×C/2, where A, B, C are orthogonal dimensions in cm.

   APACHE II
      Acute Physiology and Chronic Health Evaluation II; severity-of-illness score used for mortality prediction in ICU.

   ATLS
      Advanced Trauma Life Support; structured approach to trauma care (ABCDE primary survey).

   CTAS
      Canadian Triage and Acuity Scale; five-level ED triage system (Level I most urgent to Level V least urgent).

   CURB-65
      Clinical rule for severity of community-acquired pneumonia: Confusion, Urea, Respiratory rate, Blood pressure, Age ≥65.

   ESI
      Emergency Severity Index; five-level ED triage algorithm (v4) based on acuity and resource needs.

   GCS
      Glasgow Coma Scale; score (3–15) for level of consciousness (eye, verbal, motor).

   HEART score
      History, ECG, Age, Risk factors, Troponin; risk stratification for chest pain.

   HRV
      Heart rate variability; variation in RR intervals; time and frequency domain metrics (SDNN, RMSSD, LF, HF).

   ISS
      Injury Severity Score; composite score from three worst AIS (Abbreviated Injury Scale) body regions.

   LACE
      Length of stay, Acuity, Comorbidity, ED visits; index for 30-day readmission risk.

   MEWS
      Modified Early Warning Score; aggregate of vital signs and AVPU for deterioration risk.

   NEWS2
      National Early Warning Score 2; UK standard for acute illness severity and escalation.

   NIHSS
      National Institutes of Health Stroke Scale; 0–42 scale for stroke severity.

   PEWS
      Pediatric Early Warning Score; early detection of deterioration in children.

   qSOFA
      Quick Sequential Organ Failure Assessment; bedside criteria (SBP ≤100, RR ≥22, altered mentation) for sepsis screening.

   RTS
      Revised Trauma Score; weighted combination of GCS, systolic BP, respiratory rate.

   SOFA
      Sequential Organ Failure Assessment; six organ systems, score 0–24; used in sepsis and critical care.

   TRISS
      Trauma and Injury Severity Score; probability of survival from RTS, ISS, age, and mechanism.

   TBSA
      Total body surface area; percentage of body burned (e.g. Rule of Nines, Lund-Browder).
